Research in many areas of design forms the backbone of KSR - CEM.

Investigations into the conceptual and theoretical foundations of design process and methodologies have had implications on the way design is being thought at KSR-CEM. Investigations into social and cultural aspects have had a influence on design of many of the products and applications that have been undertaken in the department.
